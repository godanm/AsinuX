## Full Description
```
AsinuX is a fast-paced, strategic multiplayer card game inspired by the classic Donkey card game played across South Asia and beyond.

🃏 HOW TO PLAY
Every player is dealt a hand of cards. Each round, players take turns leading tricks — you must follow the lead suit if you can. Run out of cards first to escape! The last player holding cards becomes the Donkey 🫏 and loses the round.

Watch out for cuts — if you don't have the lead suit, you can play any card to cut the trick, sending all the cards back to you!

⚡ FEATURES
• Instant matchmaking — jump into a game in seconds
• Smart AI opponents that adapt to your playstyle
• Beautiful card table with smooth animations
• Live turn indicators and trick animations
• Points system — start with 10,000 points, earn more by finishing first
• Leaderboard — climb the ranks against players worldwide
• Stats tracking — wins, rounds played, points earned
• Custom avatar — pick your icon and colour
• Works offline against bots

🏆 POINTS SYSTEM
• 1st place: +100 pts
• 2nd place: +60 pts
• 3rd place: +30 pts
• Donkey: -50 pts

Can you escape the Donkey and climb to the top?

Download AsinuX and find out!
```
